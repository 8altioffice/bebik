Getting Started

What is Bebik?

Bebik is a comprehensive baby tracking app that helps you monitor your baby's activities, growth, and development. Track feeding sessions, sleep patterns, diaper changes, medication, temperature, and growth measurements all in one place.

How do I create my first baby profile?

  1. Open the app and tap "Create Profile" (or "+" button)
  2. Enter your baby's name, birth date, and gender
  3. Optionally add a profile photo from your photo library
  4. Tap "Save" to create the profile

Is the app free?

Yes! Bebik offers a free version with basic features including:

  • Basic activity tracking (feeding, sleep, diaper changes, etc.)
  • Basic profile management (1 profile)
  • Basic history viewing

Premium features require a subscription for advanced analytics, PDF reports, multiple profiles (up to 7), and more.

How do I subscribe?

  1. Open the app and navigate to Settings
  2. Tap on "Premium" section
  3. Select your preferred subscription plan
  4. Complete the purchase through Apple's App Store

My premium features aren't unlocking after purchase

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Wait a few minutes - Sometimes there's a brief delay in subscription activation
  2. Restart the app - Close the app completely and reopen it
  3. Check your internet connection - Subscription verification requires an internet connection
  4. Restore purchases - Go to Settings ‚Üí Premium ‚Üí Restore Purchases
  5. Check your Apple ID - Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase

If issues persist, contact support with your purchase receipt.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find "Bebik" in the list
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No refunds are provided for the current billing period.

How do I get a refund?

Refund requests must be submitted through Apple's App Store support system:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Bebik purchase
  4. Select "Request a refund" and follow the prompts

The Service Provider does not process refunds directly. Apple's refund policy applies to all purchases.

My subscription shows as expired but I just renewed

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Premium ‚Üí Restore Purchases
  3. Restart the app
  4. If still showing expired, wait 15-30 minutes for Apple's servers to update

If the issue persists after 24 hours, contact support with your purchase receipt.

iCloud Sync

What is iCloud Sync?

iCloud Sync allows you to sync your baby's data across all your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, etc.) using your personal iCloud account.

How do I enable iCloud Sync?

iCloud Sync is automatically enabled when you:

  1. Are signed in to iCloud on your device
  2. Have iCloud Drive enabled
  3. Have sufficient iCloud storage space

The app will automatically sync your data when connected to the internet.

My data isn't syncing between devices

Check these common issues:

  1. iCloud Account: Ensure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all devices
    • Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Check Apple ID at top
  2. iCloud Storage: Verify you have enough iCloud storage
    • Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Check available storage
  3. Internet Connection: Ensure both devices are connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data
  4. Sync Status: Check Settings ‚Üí iCloud Sync ‚Üí Sync Status in the app
  5. Wait time: Initial sync can take several minutes. Wait at least 10-15 minutes before troubleshooting
  6. Force Sync: Close and reopen the app on both devices

Can I disable iCloud Sync?

Yes, but this will stop syncing. Your data will remain on each device locally. To disable:

  1. Settings ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Turn off iCloud Drive (affects all apps)
  2. Or simply delete the app - data stored locally will remain

Note: Disabling iCloud sync means your data won't sync across devices.

How do I delete all iCloud data?

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: This permanently deletes all your data from iCloud and cannot be undone.

  1. Open Settings in the app
  2. Scroll to "iCloud Sync" section
  3. Tap "Delete All iCloud Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion

This only deletes data from iCloud. Local data on your device will remain unless you also delete the app.

Will my data be safe in iCloud?

Yes. Your data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. The data is encrypted and only accessible through your Apple ID. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Data Management

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored in two places:

  1. Locally on your device - All data is stored on your iPhone/iPad using SwiftData
  2. iCloud (optional) - If iCloud sync is enabled, data is also stored in your personal iCloud account

How do I backup my data?

Automatic Backup:

  • If iCloud Sync is enabled, your data is automatically backed up to iCloud
  • You can also use iTunes/Finder to backup your device, which includes app data

Manual Backup:

  • Generate a PDF report (Premium feature) for important records
  • Take screenshots of important data

I accidentally deleted data. Can I recover it?

Unfortunately, deleted data cannot be recovered once removed. However:

  • If you have iCloud sync enabled, check other devices - they may still have the data
  • If you generated PDF reports, those may contain the information you need

Tip: Always double-check before deleting entries, as deletion is permanent.

How do I export my data?

Premium users can generate PDF reports:

  1. Go to the section you want to export (History, Growth, Analytics)
  2. Look for the PDF/share button
  3. Select what to include in the report
  4. Generate and share the PDF

Can I import data from another app?

Currently, Bebik does not support importing data from other apps. If you need to transfer data, you'll need to manually enter it or contact support for assistance.

Live Activities

What are Live Activities?

Live Activities show real-time information on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island (iPhone 14 Pro and later). Bebik uses Live Activities to display sleep timer information.

My Live Activity isn't showing

Live Activities require:

  • iOS 16.1 or later
  • iPhone (not available on iPad)
  • Live Activities enabled in Settings

To enable Live Activities:

  1. Settings ‚Üí Face ID & Passcode (or Touch ID & Passcode) ‚Üí Allow Access When Locked ‚Üí Live Activities
  2. Settings ‚Üí Bebik ‚Üí Turn on Live Activities

If still not working:

  1. Restart your device
  2. Ensure the sleep timer is actually running
  3. Check that you're running iOS 16.1 or later
  4. Make sure Live Activities aren't disabled in Focus modes

Live Activity disappeared

Live Activities automatically end when:

  • The sleep timer stops
  • The app is force-closed (may end activity)
  • Your device restarts

To restart, simply start a new sleep timer session.

Can I use Live Activities on iPad?

Live Activities are not available on iPad. They're only supported on iPhone devices running iOS 16.1 or later.

Profile Management

How many profiles can I create?

  • Free users: 1 profile
  • Premium users: Up to 7 profiles

How do I add another profile?

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Profile Manager (or tap the profile icon)

How do I switch between profiles?

  1. Tap the profile icon/button at the top of the home screen
  2. Select the profile you want to view
  3. All data will update to show that profile's information

Can I delete a profile?

Yes, but this will permanently delete all data for that profile:

  1. Settings ‚Üí Profile Manager
  2. Swipe left on the profile or tap "Edit"
  3. Tap "Delete Profile"
  4. Confirm deletion

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: This action cannot be undone. All data for that profile will be permanently deleted.

How do I edit a profile?

  1. Settings ‚Üí Profile Manager
  2. Tap on the profile you want to edit
  3. Make your changes
  4. Save

Can I change my baby's photo?

Yes:

  1. Settings ‚Üí Profile Manager
  2. Tap on the profile
  3. Tap the profile photo
  4. Select a new photo from your library or take a new photo

Note: You'll need to grant photo library access when prompted.

Growth percentiles show how your baby's measurements compare to other babies of the same age and gender, based on WHO (World Health Organization) growth charts. Percentiles help you understand if your baby is growing at a healthy rate.

Troubleshooting

The app crashes or freezes

  1. Force close and reopen the app
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Free up storage space on your device
  5. Check iOS version - Ensure you're running iOS 15.0 or later

If crashes persist, contact support with:

  • Device model (e.g., iPhone 14 Pro)
  • iOS version (Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí About)
  • Steps to reproduce the crash

The app won't open

  1. Restart your device
  2. Delete and reinstall the app (‚ö†Ô∏è Make sure iCloud sync is enabled first to preserve data)
  3. Check device storage - Ensure you have at least 500MB free
  4. Update iOS to the latest version

My data disappeared

Check these:

  1. Wrong profile selected - Switch profiles to see if data is there
  2. Date filter - Check if a date filter is applied in History
  3. iCloud sync - If using multiple devices, check the other device
  4. App was deleted/reinstalled - If you didn't have iCloud sync enabled, data may be lost

If data is truly missing:

  1. Check Settings ‚Üí iCloud Sync ‚Üí Sync Status
  2. Restore from iCloud backup if available
  3. Contact support for assistance

I can't add a photo to a profile

Check permissions:

  1. Settings ‚Üí Bebik ‚Üí Photos ‚Üí Select "All Photos" or "Selected Photos"
  2. If permission was denied, reset it in Settings ‚Üí Bebik ‚Üí Reset Permissions

Other issues:

  • Ensure you have photos in your library
  • Check that you have enough storage space
  • Try restarting the app

The app is slow or laggy

  1. Close other apps to free up memory
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check storage space - Low storage can slow down the app
  4. Limit data - If you have thousands of entries, consider archiving old data
  5. Update the app to the latest version

I'm getting error messages

Common errors and solutions:

  • "Network error" ‚Üí Check internet connection, try again later
  • "Purchase failed" ‚Üí Check Apple ID, payment method, try again
  • "Sync failed" ‚Üí Check iCloud settings, internet connection
  • "Permission denied" ‚Üí Check app permissions in Settings ‚Üí Bebik

If you see a specific error message, note it down and contact support.

The language isn't changing

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Language
  2. Select your preferred language
  3. Restart the app
  4. If still not working, restart your device

Supported languages: English, Turkish, Spanish, German, and more.

My reminders aren't working

Check these:

  1. Notification permissions: Settings ‚Üí Bebik ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Enable
  2. Do Not Disturb: Ensure DND isn't blocking notifications
  3. Focus modes: Check if Focus modes are silencing notifications
  4. Reminder settings: Verify reminders are enabled in Settings ‚Üí Reminders
  5. Device time: Ensure device time is set correctly

PDF reports aren't generating

Troubleshooting steps:

  1. Ensure you have a Premium subscription
  2. Check internet connection (required for PDF generation)
  3. Try again after a few minutes
  4. Restart the app
  5. Ensure you have enough storage space

If issues persist, contact support.
